The dataset includes two columns:

  • DATE: This column represents the date in "YYYY-MM-DD" format, showing each month's data.
  • CPIAUCSL_PC1: This column represents the annual percent change in the Consumer Price Index (CPI), which is a measure of inflation.

Each row corresponds to a specific month, showing the inflation rate at that time.
